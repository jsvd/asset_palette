# Western Theme Asset Research - OpenGameArt.org

Research conducted: 2026-02-22
Goal: Find CC0/public domain western-themed sprites for "Duel at Noon" western dueling game

## Requirements

- Gunslinger characters (idle, draw, shoot poses)
- Desert/town backgrounds or tiles
- Western props (barrels, signs, tumbleweeds)
- License: Must be CC0 or public domain

---

## Best CC0 Western Assets Found

### 1. Cowboy Character Sprite (RECOMMENDED)

**URL:** https://opengameart.org/content/cowboy

**License:** CC0 (Public Domain) - Confirmed CC0 after license.txt conflict was resolved

**Download:** [Cowboy 4 HiRes.zip](https://opengameart.org/sites/default/files/Cowboy%204%20HiRes_0.zip) (28.1 KB)

**Art Style:** Pixel art, 2D sidescroller character

**Animations Included (8 cycles):**
- Idle without gun
- Idle with gun
- Walk without gun
- Walk with gun
- Jump without gun
- Jump with gun
- Shoot while standing
- Shoot while jumping

**Assessment:** Excellent fit for a dueling game. Has all the essential gunslinger poses needed: idle, draw (transition between idle states), and shoot. High-res pixel art quality.

**Note:** Creator mentions a larger "Western Package" available from their website with tileset, 7 additional character sprites, and parallax backgrounds.
